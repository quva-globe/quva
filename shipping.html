Pricing & Delivery Policy

1. Pricing Model

Quva Technologies operates on a custom-quote basis. Pricing for mobile applications, web platforms, and API integrations is determined after a thorough requirements analysis. A detailed invoice will be provided before work begins.

2. Delivery of Digital Services

Quva is a digital software development agency. We do not ship physical products. Therefore, no physical shipping or delivery timelines apply.

3. Milestone Delivery

All deliverables (source code, API keys, APKs, Web Hosting deployments) are delivered electronically via secure links, GitHub repositories, or cloud deployment servers. Delivery timelines for each digital milestone will be outlined in your specific project contract.

If you have any issues receiving your digital assets, please contact us immediately at contact@quva.in.
